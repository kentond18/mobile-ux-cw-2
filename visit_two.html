City Hall

The city hall is the headquarters of the Greater London Authority, which is located between London Bridge and the Tower of London.The shape of the building is interesting and this has been compared with different things such as misshapen egg, onion and motorcycle helmet.When the building lights up, the interior can be seen differently and it will be shining through the steely skin.

Tower Bridge

It is a well known movable bridge which stands over the river thames.This takes 61 seconds to open it and it opens around 1000 times a year.The visitors will have the chance to experience a birds eye view from 42 metres above the river.

Sky Garden
